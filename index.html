No Shop Sherlock: Google Search w/o Ecommerce Results

No Shop Sherlock is a Google search filter that strives to eliminate as much ecommerce content as possible, along with other results that tend to clutter results. Amazon, eBay, Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest are eliminated as much as possible, with other URL patterns used to minimize shopping and ecommerce-related content.

Just enter your query and hit the Go button and your completed search URL will open in a new tab. Note that the eliminated keywords and sites use up much of Google's query limit, so keep your query to six words or less.
